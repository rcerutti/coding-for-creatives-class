Ryan Cerutti Introduction

My name is Ryan Cerutti and I am currently attending Miami Ad School as an Art Direction student.
Originating from San Diego, California, I decided to leave the sun and sand and trade it in for concrete
and seasons. Now living in New York City for 3 years I have taken another leap of faith and joined Miami
Ad School to pursue a career in advertising after spending so many other years of my life working jobs
that I never liked, causing me to resign many after only a year or two, leaving a trail of 16 to 20
different jobs in my wake.

When looking toward my future I had no choice but to look at the past and see what, if anything, those
jobs had in common which would lead me into my next step that could be permanent and sustaining for myself
and my family. During those formative years there was only one constant in my life and that was my creative
side. No matter the income, schedule, or mental tax any of my jobs took on me; I seemed to have always had
the motivation to create. Collaborating with friends to make short films, build web sites, film web series,
draw comic strips and books, and write blogs. I found there was no end to what medium I wanted to put my
creative stamp onto. Even though I found time to create, I never thought I could make a living doing any of
the things I enjoyed so I fell out of touch with the innovation and pushed more into what I felt was logical
for me to sustain a living.

After living here in the city for a while I began to question what it was I was doing here exactly to
which I began to realize the opportunity that New York has to offer and how I was squandering my time here.
So I decided to reverse engineer what I wanted to do. I searched the internet for jobs that were creative which
led me to read about an Art Director position. Wondering how that was an actual job, I looked to see what I
needed to do educationally to achieve this job title. I kept seeing Miami Ad School pop up. I got my information,
sat in on a class and fell in love with what was being talked about and saw there were people just like me all
over the place who I could connect to that shared my same passion and thought process.

After that, I enrolled.

I hope with this new path I will be able to sustain a full future of creativity and would like to be the best
I can possibly be in my new positions to come. I want to use my experiences to give me the edge over others
where I can stand out as an award winner and a person others can rely on and trust. I would like to eventually
become a creative director and oversee many projects and ideas from concept to reality. That is what I have
enjoyed doing in the past with my free time, I just can't believe I will get the chance to do it for a living.
